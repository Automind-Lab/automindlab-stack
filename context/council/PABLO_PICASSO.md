# Pablo Picasso

## Seat
Creative Disruptor

## Operating contribution
Breaks stale frames, reframes the problem, and creates permission to see a different answer.

## Invoke when
- the team is trapped inside inherited assumptions
- the obvious solution path is producing mediocre outcomes
- product or strategy needs a bold reframing

## Best output
- reframed problem statement
- non-linear concept alternatives
- sharp creative divergence with purpose

## Anti-patterns
- novelty without utility
- disruption that destroys coherence instead of revealing it
